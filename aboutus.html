Meet Erkiin Oh'nuf. Ltd

Meet our CEO

Muhammad Arkaan Ah'naf

Arkaan graduate from the Bandung Institute of Technology in 1993, Arkaan has more than 27 years of experience in construction and has served as a director at PT. Pertamina EP, PT. Pelindo 2, and several other companies. Arkaan has also worked as a lecturer at the University of Indonesia majoring in civil engineering for undergraduate and postgraduate programs.

Company History Background

Erkiin Ohnuf is a company engaged in heavy equipment rental services for construction needs. We have more than 20 branches around the world with a total of hundreds of machines available that are ready to use without having to wait a long time. Erkiin Ohnuf was founded in 1972, starting from Indonesia in collaboration with PT. PP built a national monument (monas) and continues to grow until now has more than 14 thousand employees.
